# 🗓️ 2026 Interactive Wall Calendar ✨

A high-fidelity digital recreation of a classic physical wall calendar, built with **React**, **Framer Motion**, and **Tailwind CSS**. 

This project goes beyond standard grid-heavy digital planners by prioritizing the tactile and aesthetic experience of flipping through a real paper calendar. It captures the essence of a physical planner hanging on your wall—complete with monthly artwork, handwritten-style notes, and a dedicated area for quick doodles. 

---

## ✨ Core Features

###  Authentic Wall Calendar Experience
The interface is designed to resemble a spiral-bound physical planner. It features high-quality monthly imagery that shifts with the seasons, creating a distinct "vibe" for every month of the year. 

###  Multi-Layered Planning
* **One Full Year:** Seamlessly navigate from the decorative cover page through all twelve months of 2026. 
* **Holiday & Event Marker:** Click on specific dates to label them as holidays or special events, adding a personal layer to your view. 
* **Date Range Selector:** A fluid selection tool allows you to highlight spans of time, perfect for tracking trips, deadlines, or project durations. 

###  Personal Expression Areas
* **Lined Notes Section:** Every month includes a note-taking area with a ruled-paper background. Store general monthly goals or click a specific date for daily reminders. 
* **Scribble Pad:** Mimic the habit of doodling in the margins. A built-in canvas allows for freehand drawing and sketching using the mouse or touch. 

###  Aesthetic & Smooth Transitions
* **Fluid Motion:** Hardware-accelerated transitions avoid blurriness, ensuring a crisp and smooth "sliding" effect during month changes. 
* **Theme Customization:** A built-in theme selector lets you change the primary accent color (Pink, Orange, Blue, Green, or Original) to match your mood or desk setup. 

---

## ✨ Technical Highlights

* **State Persistence:** All notes, events, and scribbles are saved to **Local Storage**. Your data remains intact even after refreshing the browser. 
* **Responsive Design:** Fully adaptive layout that scales for a compact mobile experience while remaining expansive on desktops.
* **Canvas Integration:** Uses the HTML5 Canvas API for the scribble area, supporting both mouse and touch events. 

---

## ✨ Usage

1.  **Opening the Calendar:** Click the "Open Calendar" prompt on the 2026 cover page. 
2.  **Navigation:** Use the arrow buttons next to the month name to flip through the year. 
3.  **Taking Notes:** Click a date to see notes for that specific day, or use the **"BACK TO MONTHLY VIEW"** button to return to general objectives. 
4.  **Scribbling:** Use the square box in the bottom right to draw. Click **"CLR"** to wipe the canvas clean. 
5.  **Adding Events:** Select a date and click **"+ ADD EVENT"** to mark a specific holiday or milestone.
